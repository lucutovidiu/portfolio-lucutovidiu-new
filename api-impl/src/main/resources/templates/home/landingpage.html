A little intro about myself

My Lifelong Passion for Technology

My journey into the world of technology began at the age of 7, a time when computers were still a rarity. However, my first glimpse of a computer on television ignited a deep passion within me. I took my first steps into the world of coding during high school, and I've never looked back since. My academic pursuits led me to university, where I continued to explore the fascinating realms of programming and technology. The more I delved into this world, the more I fell in love with it.

My insatiable curiosity led me to constantly seek out new technologies and frameworks. I am always eager to improve and expand my knowledge and skills. A few years ago, I embarked on a journey with JavaScript, focusing on Node.js and React. However, about a year ago, I intensified my commitment to development, diving headfirst into Angular 2+ and Java on the backend. I can honestly say that I am absolutely enamored with both, and my passion for technology continues to grow stronger with each passing day.
